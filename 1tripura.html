TRIPURA




Tripura is a hilly state in northeast India,Bordered on 3 sides of Bangladesh. Its capital is AGARTALA.
It is the third smallest state in the country and the seventh least populous state.
Tripura is anisolated hilly region in the country, with various indigenous
peoples-Tribes-accounting for a significant portion of the population.





Climate condition in Tripura
The warmest months in Tripura are April and May,the coolest month is January.The state
has a Tropical Savanna climate and receives seasonal heavy rain from the south west Monsoon.
